About Us

We are Team Bangladesh Red Cross

A crazy group of people to inspire a nation

A passionate group of people who wishes to inspire a nation and beyond. Bangladesh
Red Cross is an educational voluntary organization that originated from Bangladesh.
Bangladesh Red Cross has been operated by 40 professionals and with help of
thousands of volunteers. Our mission is to globally foster philanthropic works.
By designing innovative and inspiring programs, our goal is to engage students
and professionals to work together to educate, empower and lift the
underprivileged. One of our endeavors is to provide free education,
educational materials and support.
